The Company - coreDev Solutions, Inc.

History

Wonder how coreDev Solutions, Incorporated initiated its name in the Accounting and IT world?

Twenty Five years ago, the very first version of our accounting software was developed and it was named as "iAccs" (Integrated Accounting System) in which it started as a DOS-based application. It has greatly helped Cooperatives and Rural Banks automate their Credit and Savings operation while enjoying integration into the Accounting System.

The rapid advancement in technology and the ever growing needs of our clients inspired us to further develop the system. In the year 2013, we launched the newest version of the system, we called it "iAccs 2013". We have evolved from being just a software provider into a service-oriented solutions provider. Thus the need to serve our clients and our aims to drastically broaden our horizons, brought the birth of coreDev, Solutions Incorported that serves its clients 24/7.

We offer every solution and provide the best service to our clients nationwide. We aim for customer satisfaction and innovation in developing safe, reliable and stable services to our clients, making every client as our only priority. CoreDev Solutions Incorporated is not just specialized in Accounting System, we also offer customized software development, hardware, network infrastructure deployment, and technical consultancy..

Department Assignment

Description and Scope of Responsibility

We trainees were directly assigned at the company's Research and Development Department, where we were given projects to work on during our training period. My team was asked to come up with a web system that will help the company employees vote and decide the event for their monthly Rest and Recreation.
Thus the development of the 'R&R Portal'

Summary of Weekly Activities

Review and Research

To start the development of the Rest&Recreation Portal, we first need to know the proper knowledge and languages to make the project possible. The company's programmer guided us to what to learn and provide us with learning materials, such as video tutorials and references.

The results of the review and research was fruitful, we were able to learn new programming languages, discovered web development frameworks and acquired skills. We also reviewed the lessons taught at school.

Back-End Programming

Back-End Web Developer
A back-end web developer is responsible for server-side web application logic and integration of the work front-end web developers do. Back-end developers usually write web services and APIs used by front-end developers and mobile application developers.

I was tasked to the back-end of the website, where I programmed the components and modules of the project. I also created and maintained the logic, classes, database and processes to ensure that the R&R Portal is functional.

Version Control

As one of the knowledged taught by the programmers to us, we applied version control to our project. Where we almost pushed updates to our repository in gitHub at the end of everday and pulled it in the next day. Git has been very helpful to us, due to the fact that the data integrity of the source codes is assured and the workflow of the project is flexible.

Company Problems

As perceived by the Company

The company is always in need of new software programmers and implementers. And as of the writing of this portofolio, the hiring of the positions are still open.

As perceived by the Trainee

Fortunately, I have no problems by the company itself. I was blessed to be part of the Research and Development Department, where I was given a chance to learn new things and realize that in this industry, being a lifelong learner is indeed a plus.

Findings and Analysis

During my training period, the employees are all approachable and is ready to answer any question and problems you have encountered. I would totally recommend anyone who is willing to learn and discover new things to apply for OJT position at coreDev.

In my opinion, the list of companies that an OJT-Trainee can apply to must have an addition info about what the company TRULY wants from the trainee. The info can range from 'is the applicant willing to learn and can work with less supervision' to 'is willing to do what the higher-ups ask you, no matter what the task'.
